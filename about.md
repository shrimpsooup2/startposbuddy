# StartPos Buddy

Link a level with its StartPos version and hot-swap between them with one key.

## Linking two levels
1. Open a level's page and press the **SP** button (works on online level pages and local level pages).
2. Choose **Mark**.
3. Open the other version's page, press **SP** again, choose **Link**.

## Switching in-game
Press the **Switch Level Version** keybind (default <cy>Tab</cy>) while playing either version to jump straight into the other one.

## Auto-creating a StartPos version
While playing any level (practice mode recommended so you can reach the exact spot), press the **Add StartPos Here** keybind (default <cy>E</cy>):

- Creates a local copy named "&lt;level&gt; SP" with a StartPos at your current position (matching your gamemode, speed, size and dual state), and links it to the level automatically.
- Each time you add another StartPos, a **new copy** is made that keeps all the previous StartPoses plus the new one (named "&lt;level&gt; SP 2", "&lt;level&gt; SP 3", ...), and the link points at the newest copy. Older copies are left untouched so nothing is lost.
- Enable **Replace Existing StartPos** in the mod settings if you instead want each add to move the single StartPos rather than stack them.

Both keybinds are rebindable from the mod's settings (the gear icon on the mod's page) or from the game's in-game **Keybindings** menu. Note: local level copies are written to your save when GD closes normally.
